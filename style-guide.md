## Brand Voice & Style Guide: Dundle (German Market)

This guide outlines the tone, structure, and linguistic requirements for converting or creating content for the German market.

### 1. Tone & Personality
* **Enthusiastic & Urgent:** Use exclamation marks to convey excitement (e.g., *"Der Bestellprozess ist sicher, schnell und einfach!"*).
* **Reassuring & Secure:** Consistently emphasize safety and anonymity. Use keywords like **"sicher und anonym,"** **"geschützt,"** and **"volle Kostenkontrolle."**
* **Solution-Oriented:** Focus on speed and convenience. Address pain points (like not wanting to share bank details) and position dundle as the obvious solution.
    > *Phrase to use:* "Dann bist du bei dundle genau richtig!"
* **Tech-Savvy & Modern:** Comfortable with digital products, online transactions, and modern payment methods.

### 2. Voice & Language
* **Address:** Informal Second Person Singular (**"du"**). Never use "Sie."
* **Direct & Action-Oriented:** Use strong imperative verbs.
    * *Examples:* "Hol dir jetzt," "Kaufe dein," "Besuche," "Gib ein."
* **Conversational:** Create a connection using rhetorical questions.
    * *Pattern:* "Möchtest du...?" followed immediately by the solution.
* **Clarity:** Explain technical concepts clearly with practical examples. Provide alternative terminology in natural German flow (e.g., *"Neosurf-Ticket (manchmal auch Neosurf-Gutschein genannt)"*).

---

### 3. Formatting & Style
The visual presentation must be scannable and organized.

* **Bold Text:** Use strategically for key benefits and Calls to Action (CTA).
    * *Example:* **Sicher & anonym bezahlen ohne Bankkonto oder Kreditkarte**
* **Lists:** Use bullet points, often utilizing checkmarks (✓) for visual appeal in "Benefits" sections.
* **Step-by-Step:** Numbered instructions should always conclude with **"Fertig!"** or **"Das war's!"** to give a sense of accomplishment.
* **Conventions:**
    * Use **"bzw."** for alternatives.
    * Use **"dt."** for translations of English UI terms.
    * **Currency:** Place "€" after the number with a space (e.g., `5 €`).
    * **Numbers:** "3 Monate" (omit "lang").

---

### 4. Content Structure
German pages follow a strict hierarchy and are generally **10–20% longer** than English versions due to compound words and detailed explanations (800–1,500 words).

| Section | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1. Main Headline** | Format: `[Product] kaufen` + Delivery method (e.g., "Codes sofort per E-Mail geliefert"). |
| **2. Subheadline (H2)** | Value proposition and key benefits. |
| **3. Opening** | Starts with "Möchtest du...?" followed by dundle as the solution. (40–60 words). |
| **4. "Was ist..."** | Explains the product clearly. |
| **5. "Vorteile"** | Dedicated section with bullet points (✓) listing benefits. (approx. 100–150 words). |
| **6. Context** | Additional product details (varies by product). |
| **7. FAQ** | "Häufig gestellte Fragen" – Direct questions (no "How do I"), conversational answers. |
| **8. Closing** | Standardized "dundle in Deutschland" paragraph (approx. 60 words). |

---

### 5. Key Content Patterns
Ensure these elements are present in the copy:

* **Payment Flexibility:** Always mention specific methods: **PayPal, Apple Pay, Klarna Sofortüberweisung**.
* **No Credit Card:** Emphasize purchasing **"ohne Kreditkarte."**
* **Speed:** Highlight "Sofort per E-Mail," "direkt," "innerhalb von Sekunden."
* **Regional Restrictions:** Must be addressed in the FAQ with specific currency info (e.g., "auf €-Basis").
* **Loyalty:** Mention the **dundle Coins** program in benefit sections.

---

### 6. Meta Information Requirements

#### **Title Tag**
* **Length:** 50–70 characters
* **Pattern:** `[Product] kaufen | Sofort-Code per E-Mail`
* **Variation:** `[Product] kaufen | Sofort-Code per E-Mail | [Benefit]`

#### **Meta Description**
* **Length:** 140–160 characters
* **Pattern:** `[Product] [kaufen/bestellen] [beim offiziellen Vertriebspartner / online] ✓[Benefit 1] ✓[Benefit 2] – Bezahle mit PayPal, [payment method] und mehr`
* **Mandatory:** Must include checkmarks (✓), payment methods (especially PayPal), and strong action verbs.# Style Guide
